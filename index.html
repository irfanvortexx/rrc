About Our College

Rajapalayam Rajus College, founded in 1973, is the only Government-aided co-educational college within 30 km of Rajapalayam. For over 50 years it has offered quality higher education to rural and under-privileged youth.

The 52-acre campus lies at the foot of the Western Ghats and is affiliated to Madurai Kamaraj University. The college is recognized by the UGC under sections 2F & 12B.

Starting with Pre-University courses in 1973, it soon added UG and PG programs such as B.Com., B.A. History, B.Sc. Sciences, M.Com., and M.A. History, and later introduced Computer Science, Commerce, Management and Ph.D. research programmes.

Today the college continues to be a centre of academic excellence and holistic education in southern Tamil Nadu.
